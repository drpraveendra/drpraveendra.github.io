## Welcome

I am an Assistant Professor of Mathematics working in the area of **inventory control, supply chain optimization, and intelligent metaheuristic algorithms**. My research focuses on developing **mathematical models and optimization techniques for real-world decision systems involving deteriorating items, pricing policies, and sustainability constraints.**

---

## 🔬 Research Interests

• Inventory Control
• Supply Chain Management
• Metaheuristic Optimization
• Optimal Control
• Sustainable Inventory Systems
