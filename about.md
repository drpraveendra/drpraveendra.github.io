# About

Dr. Praveendra Singh is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics at Government College Kherwara, Rajasthan, India.

He completed his Ph.D. in Mathematics from the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee with a thesis titled:

"Nature-Inspired Optimization for Inventory Control of Non-instantaneously Deteriorating Items."

His research lies at the intersection of operations research, inventory modeling, and intelligent optimization techniques.

His work focuses on developing mathematical models and metaheuristic algorithms for complex supply chain systems involving deterioration, pricing policies, and sustainability constraints.
